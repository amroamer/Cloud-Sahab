Below is a focused KPI set for each Ajwaa service group, tuned for a regulator (GACA) and aligned with good licensing/permit KPI practice.

1. Licensing and personnel services
Pilot licenses (issuance, renewal, validations, conversions)
PL‑01 – Pilot license applications received (by type)
Count of applications by license type (ATPL, CPL, PPL, validation, conversion) per period.

PL‑02 – Pilot licenses issued / renewed on time (%)
Approved before current license expiry ÷ total renewals due in period.
​

PL‑03 – Average processing time – pilot license (days)
Mean calendar days from complete application to decision, by license type.
​

PL‑04 – First‑time approval rate – pilot licenses (%)
Applications approved without resubmission ÷ total decided applications.
​

PL‑05 – Digital completion rate – pilot licensing (%)
Applications fully completed through Ajwaa (no manual back‑and‑forth) ÷ total applications.

PL‑06 – Revenue from pilot licensing fees (SAR)
Sum of fees invoiced for issuance/renewal/validation/conversion (can be reconciled to the GACA charges schedule).
​

Cabin crew licenses and crew member cards
CC‑01 – Cabin crew licenses/cards active
Number of valid cabin crew licenses/ID cards in force.

CC‑02 – Cabin crew license renewals on time (%)
Renewed before expiry ÷ licenses expiring in period.
​

CC‑03 – Average processing time – cabin crew license (days)
Complete application to decision.
​

CC‑04 – Applications per 1,000 active cabin crew
Total new + renewal + reissue applications in period ÷ active cabin crew ÷ 1,000; proxy for workload.
​

ATCOs and other aviation personnel (maintenance, dispatch, etc.)
AP‑01 – Active licenses by personnel category
Counts by ATCO, maintenance engineer, dispatcher, etc.

AP‑02 – License non‑compliance rate (%)
Number of detected cases of operating with expired/suspended license ÷ spot checks/audits.
​

AP‑03 – Average processing time – personnel license (days)
For ATCO and other license types.
​

AP‑04 – Percentage of licenses renewed online (%)
Renewals processed entirely through Ajwaa ÷ total renewals.

2. Flight and operation permits
Single flight permits (one‑off commercial or charter)
FP‑01 – Single flight permit applications received
Count by type (commercial charter, business jet, humanitarian, diplomatic).

FP‑02 – On‑time issuance rate – single flight permits (%)
Permits issued within service standard (e.g., 24 hours) ÷ total applications.

FP‑03 – Average processing time – single flight permit (hours)
Submission to permit issuance.

FP‑04 – Rejection / modification rate – single flight permits (%)
Applications that require change or are rejected ÷ total decided applications.
​

Annual / multiple flight permits (scheduled & non‑scheduled)
FP‑05 – Active annual / multiple flight permits
Number of valid permits by operator type (scheduled, non‑scheduled, charter).

FP‑06 – Renewal on‑time rate – annual permits (%)
Renewed before expiry ÷ total due renewals.
​

FP‑07 – Average processing time – annual permit (days)
Complete application to decision.
​

FP‑08 – Revenue from flight permit fees (SAR)
Based on the GACA service charge schedule (overflight/landing/annual permit fee codes).
​

Overflight and landing permits for foreign operators
FP‑09 – Overflight permits issued (foreign operators)
Total permits and breakdown (one‑off vs annual; commercial vs private).

FP‑10 – Landing permits issued (foreign operators)
Same, but landing permissions.

FP‑11 – Compliance violations – overflight/landing permits
Flights operated without valid permit ÷ flights sampled (from ATC data).
​

FP‑12 – Average time to clear overflight for compliant applications (hours)
SLA measure for CWC/Oboor channel.
​

3. Economic and regulatory approvals
Economic licenses for airlines and air operators (AOC‑related)
EL‑01 – Active economic licenses by carrier type
Number of licenses for national scheduled, non‑scheduled, cargo, low‑cost, etc.

EL‑02 – Time to issue new economic license (months)
Approval date minus complete application date.
​

EL‑03 – License renewal success rate (%)
Renewed licenses ÷ expiring licenses; track by reason for non‑renewal (non‑compliance, voluntary exit).
​

EL‑04 – Economic license violations detected
Operators offering services without valid economic license.
​

EL‑05 – Revenue from economic licensing fees (SAR)
Sum of fees by license type (e.g., scheduled vs non‑scheduled) as per regulatory charges document.
​

Approvals for general aviation and private aviation activities
GA‑01 – Active general aviation operator approvals
Number of approved GA/BA operators (air taxi, helicopter, MRO with flight ops, etc.).

GA‑02 – New GA approvals granted per year
Shows growth of general aviation sector.

GA‑03 – Processing time – GA approval (days)
Application to decision.
​

GA‑04 – Compliance audit pass rate – GA operators (%)
Operators passing audits without major findings ÷ audited operators.
​

4. Airport, ANSP, and service‑provider services
Services for airport operators (concessions, approvals, filings)
AO‑01 – Approved airport operators / concessions
Number of airport operating entities and major concessions under GACA oversight.
​

AO‑02 – Regulatory filing compliance rate (%)
Required filings (tariffs, slot declarations, safety reports) submitted on time ÷ total required.
​

AO‑03 – Processing time – new airport concession/approval (months)
For major airport projects or operator changes.
​

AO‑04 – Share of airport operator requests processed digitally (%)
Requests handled fully via Ajwaa ÷ total requests.

Ground handling, maintenance, and other aviation service companies
SP‑01 – Number of certified service providers (by category)
Ground handling, catering, MRO, fuel, security, etc.

SP‑02 – Certification / renewal on‑time rate (%)
Certificates renewed before expiry ÷ those due in period.
​

SP‑03 – Average processing time – service‑provider approval (days)
Application to decision.
​

SP‑04 – Non‑compliance findings per audit – service providers
Average number of findings per audit; track trend to measure quality.
​

SP‑05 – Revenue from service‑provider certification fees (SAR)
Fees from ground handling, MRO, etc., per the GACA charges framework.
​

5. Cross‑cutting Ajwaa / e‑service performance KPIs
For all the above groups, you can add platform‑level KPIs:

ES‑01 – Total applications processed via Ajwaa (by service group)
Licensing, permits, economic approvals, airport services, service providers.

ES‑02 – Average end‑to‑end digital processing time (days/hours)
From online submission to final decision, by service type.

ES‑03 – Customer satisfaction score (CSAT) for Ajwaa services
Survey after completion, averaged by service category.

ES‑04 – Percentage of services fully digitized (%)
Services with no manual step ÷ all services in that category.
